Life Cycle Assessment — MIE315 Consulting Project

ISO 14040/44 · Financial Feasibility · NPV Analysis · Jan 2023 – Apr 2023

What I Did

  • Conducted a comprehensive Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) using standardized ISO 14040/44 frameworks, evaluating the environmental impacts of LED vs. CFL light bulbs across all phases: sourcing, production, use, and disposal.
  • Performed a financial feasibility analysis including capital and operational cost estimates, payback period, NPV, and sensitivity analysis to quantify the economic case alongside the environmental one.
  • Compiled and presented findings in a professional technical report, offering actionable design and implementation insights in the style of a consulting deliverable.

Outcomes

The project taught me to think about tradeoffs across a product's entire life — not just the use phase, which is where most people's intuition stops. Pairing the environmental LCA with a full financial model (NPV, sensitivity analysis) meant the recommendations were grounded in both sustainability and economic reality, which is the lens I now bring to supply chain and procurement decisions.
