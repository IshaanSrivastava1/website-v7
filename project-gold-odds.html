Automated Prediction-Market Tracking

Python · REST APIs · Matplotlib · Plotly · SMTP · Cron · Bash

View live tracker →

What I Built

  • A tool that automatically checks Polymarket's live odds on June Gold settlement prices every day, pulls in the full price history, and tracks how the odds shift over time — storing everything without creating duplicates or losing past data.
  • Generates both a quick-glance static chart and a fully interactive, zoomable chart, then emails a daily summary highlighting the biggest changes since the previous day.
  • Found and fixed a bug where the automation was silently failing whenever the laptop went to sleep — redesigned it to poll every 30 minutes with a dedup guard so it catches up without re-running completed jobs.

Thesis

I wanted an easy, free way to get daily updates on how prediction markets react to news — without having to manually check the website every morning. Instead of building a dashboard I'd forget to open, I made it come to me via email.

Next Steps

Scraping odds data from multiple prediction markets simultaneously to identify arbitrage opportunities and surface patterns in how different markets price the same event.
