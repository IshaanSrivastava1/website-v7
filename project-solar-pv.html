4th-Year Capstone — Solar PV Panel Sourcing

BOMs · RFIs · ANSYS Simulation · QA/QC · IEC Standards · Sept 2024 – Apr 2025

What I Did

  • Developed Bills of Materials (BOMs) and Requests for Information (RFIs) to support the procurement and construction of solar thermal collectors and PV panels in San Vicente, Mexico.
  • Implemented QA/QC procedures — including validating supplier certifications through recognized third parties — resulting in product compliance with IEC standards.
  • Created a functioning prototype and optimized heat transfer through the system using ANSYS simulation, resulting in a 20% cost decrease.

Outcomes

The project combined real-world procurement practice (BOMs, RFIs, supplier vetting) with hands-on engineering simulation. The QA/QC process and ANSYS optimization work directly mirrored what a sourcing or procurement team would do on a live infrastructure project.
